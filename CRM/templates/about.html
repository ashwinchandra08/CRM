About Our Customer Relationship Management DBMS Mini Project

Welcome to our Customer Relationship Management (CRM) DBMS Mini Project! This project is designed to showcase the implementation of a simple yet effective CRM system using a relational database management system (DBMS).

Objective: The primary goal of our project is to provide a platform for managing customer information, product details, and transactions efficiently. This system helps businesses keep track of their customers, products, and related transactions, facilitating better customer relationship management.

Features: Our CRM system includes features such as adding new customers, managing product details, recording transactions, and ensuring data integrity through the use of triggers in the database.

Technologies Used: The project is implemented using Flask, a web framework for Python, and integrates with a MySQL database to store and retrieve data. We have utilized Flask-SQLAlchemy for seamless database interaction.

We hope you find our CRM DBMS Mini Project informative and valuable. Thank you for exploring our work!
